Sorting Newsgroup Articles: Direct Read News offers a few different options for sorting and viewing articles that have been posted to a newsgroup. These individual toolbar tabs and their functions are described below...

  • Date:  By default, Direct Read News will arrange article lists by the most recent date first.

  • Author: Most newsgroup users will post a message with either a real or fake name, and by selecting this tab, Direct Read News will list articles in alphabetical order based on an author's name. If you enjoy reading newsgroup messages from a specific author, this is a useful sort option that will allow you to group and view all the messages that a particular person might have posted to a newsgroup.

  • Subject: When this tab is selected, Direct Read News will review the title or subject of each newsgroup message, and will sort them in alphabetical order.

  • Thread: When this tab is selected, the article list will be displayed as individual discussion "threads", where associated posts are linked together by an initial message and any follow up reply to that message. This is useful when following a specific discussion within a group.
