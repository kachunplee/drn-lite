Power Tools: This collection of Direct Read News tabs expands the functionality of DRN both in and outside the newsgroups. These tabs are described in further detail below:

  • Preferences: This area lets you create unique settings within Direct Read News, based upon your personal preferences. Subscribe to a collection of your favorite newsgroups, preset DRN to sort articles in a specific manner, increase/decrease the number of articles that will appear on a newsgroup page, create a default signature that appears within all your newsgroup postings, and more. Additional detail.

  • Find Group: Looking for a specific newsgroup title, or a collection of newsgroups related to a particular subject? This tab takes you to our Newsgroup Search Engine, which will locate individual newsgroups or a list of newsgroups, based on the complete or partial group title that type into the search engine. Once you're newsgroup(s) have been located, you simply click on a group that interests you, and you'll have instant access to the newsgroup and its contents. 

  • DRN Help: Not sure what a certain Direct Read News tab does? Unsure how to post to the newsgroup? Wondering how to subscribe to your favorite newsgroups? The "Help" tab provides you with instant access to these Direct Read News help pages.
